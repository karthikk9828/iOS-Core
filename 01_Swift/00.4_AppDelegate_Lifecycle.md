application(_:didFinishLaunchingWithOptions:)
    - Called when your app is first launched.

applicationWillEnterForeground(_:)
    - Triggered when the app is about to become active after being in the background.

applicationDidBecomeActive(_:)
    - Indicates that the app is now active and in the foreground.

applicationWillResignActive(_:)
    - Called when the app is about to enter the background.

applicationDidEnterBackground(_:)
    - Occurs when the app has entered the background.

applicationWillTerminate(_:)
    - Triggered just before the app terminates.
